Eric has been a musician most of his life. He started playing the guitar at 11, and by 15 he was in his first rock band, Vital Signs. Over the next 20 years, along with graduating from Drexel University with a B.A. in Music in 1991, Eric played guitar in a handful of great yet unknown original bands, most notably Resin, Juliet’s Wishing Well and the legendary blues band, Dr. Harmonica and Rockett 88.

Now he's a one-man band mixing his smooth voice with skilled guitar stylings for some incredible covers like you've never heard.

Live Rock Performances

I've been a performing musician since I was a child. It began with the tuba in the school band from 5th grade and then the guitar in rock bands starting in high school until 2010 when I released my first solo CD. I love making music and interacting with people so performing live in bars, restaurants and small music venues is a perfect outlet for me.
