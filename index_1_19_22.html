What a journey life is.

I've been a musician most of my life. I started playing the guitar when I was 11 and by 15, I was in my first rock band, Vital Signs. Over the next 20 years, along with graduating from Drexel University with a B.A. in Music/Marketing, I was fortunate to be in a handful of great bands, most notably Resin, Juliet's Wishing Well, Harter Attack and the legendary blues band, Dr. Harmonica and Rockett 88.

In 2010, I released my first solo CD, "All Over the Place" and in April of that year, I embarked on a six-month, cross country charity tour to support it. It was a charity tour called "Half for Humanity", which you can learn more about it here. After the tour, I continued to tour around the country, mostly around New Mexico / Colorado in the summer and Florida in the winter, touring my way back and forth.

In 2013, after a long summer of gigs and 13 shows in a row, I decided to take a break on my voice and did a 3-day silent meditation. During that time, I got the inspiration to create a unique sound healing CD, using only organic instruments, bridging the two worlds of secular and sacred music.

In May of 2015, I joyfully released the double Sound healing CD, "Good as Gold". I continue to play rock music in various venues but also offer private and group sessions using sound and music as a healing tool.

It has been proven that everything in existence is in a state of vibration. Sound is simply another word for vibration therefore we can say that sound is in everything that exists.

Wanting to be a rock and roll star from an early age, I thought it was necessary to party like one. Fortunately, my cousin saw my condition and recommended I see her friend, an herbalist and Medicine Woman, who put me on my very first "cleanse". This 10 day adventure changed my life forever! She also recommended a book called the Way of Herbs by Michael Tierra which began my studies in nutrition and healthy living. Since then, I have followed a wholistic approach to life and have helped others do the same.

This opening of consciousness then opened other doors of yoga, meditation and sound healing. In 2013 I became a Certified Sound Healer through Jonathan Goldman's Healing Sounds Course. In 2014, I enrolled in the Institute for Integrative Nutrition and became a Certified Health Coach. Then In 2015 and 2016 I got certified in Reiki I and II. Just this year, after 15 plus years of practicing yoga, I completed a 200 hour teacher training with Francois Raoult and Open Sky Yoga.

For the past 7 years I have continued my practice and have shared many of the teachings and techniques I learned over the years. I continue to work with others to help bring more balance and better health into their lives through proper nutrition and the timeless and powerful effects of sound and music.
